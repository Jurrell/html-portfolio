About Me

My name is Brian Walker. I hold a Bachelor's degree in Computer Information Systems from Southern New Hampshire University, where I gained a strong foundation in systems architecture, networking, cybersecurity, and database technologies.

I currently work as a Data Center Manager, responsible for overseeing mission‑critical infrastructure supporting enterprise IT operations. My experience includes managing physical and virtual server environments, coordinating lifecycle management for compute, storage, and network hardware, and ensuring high availability, redundancy, and environmental stability across data center facilities.

I have hands‑on expertise with a wide range of technologies, including:

  • Virtualization & Compute: VMware vSphere/ESXi, Hyper‑V, Windows Server, Linux (RHEL/Ubuntu), Dell and HPE server platforms
  • Storage Systems: Dell EMC, NetApp, HPE 3PAR/Nimble, SAN/NAS administration, tape library management (LTO)
  • Networking: Cisco switches/routers, VLAN configuration, load balancing, firewall coordination
  • Monitoring & Automation: SolarWinds, PRTG, vCenter monitoring, PowerShell scripting for task automation
  • Backup & Disaster Recovery: Veeam Backup & Replication, Commvault, tape rotation workflows, offsite retention management
  • Environmental & Facility Controls: APC and Eaton UPS systems, automated cooling/CRAC management, PDUs, and environmental alerting systems

I am passionate about creating innovative, scalable solutions to complex problems. I focus on improving operational efficiency, strengthening infrastructure resiliency, and driving modernization initiatives that support long‑term organizational growth. My approach balances hands‑on technical expertise with strategic planning, cross‑team collaboration, and a strong commitment to reliability and service quality.
